Quatrox History at a Glance

Our Journey

Our Team · 2025

2025 — Founded
Quatrox Solutions Is Born
Quatrox Solutions is established in India with a bold vision: to deliver world-class document transformation and BPO services to clients globally. The founding team unites specialists in publishing technology, digital content, and linguistic services — driven by a shared commitment to precision and quality from day one.
2025 — Launch
Full Service Suite Goes Live
Quatrox immediately launches its complete range of services — SRT captioning, ePub production, XML structuring, PDF-to-Word/Excel conversion, Copy Editing, Typesetting, and Accessibility — attracting its first wave of clients from publishing, media, e-learning, and corporate sectors.
2025 — Expansion
Voice Internationalisation Division Added
Responding to growing client demand for multilingual content, Quatrox expands into voice internationalisation — coordinating multilingual voice-over production, script adaptation, and audio localisation for clients in e-learning, corporate training, and international media.
2025 — Today
Building a Global Reputation
Within its very first year, Quatrox Solutions is already serving clients across multiple countries with offices in Thuckalay and Madurai, Tamil Nadu — building a reputation for consistency, accuracy, and genuine partnership. The journey has only just begun.
